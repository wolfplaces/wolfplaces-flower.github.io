About me

Hello, I'm Zoey Dinh, a Vietnamese mum who has spent over a decade working in the field of IT. Despite a mature career, my passion for flowers and plants has been ingrained in me since I was a kid.

Now, I am excited to embark on a new journey as a florist, combining my technological expertise with my innate love for all things floral.

The name "Wolfplaces" was derived from the nickname of my son, “Wolfie” who provides me with abundant moral support and I'm committed to putting in my best effort to this new venture. I am a Certified florist at TAFE and currently in my 2nd year with my floral business."
